Emoji.js is a plugin to convert your typing to colourful twitter emoji to use on your website or projects.

Emoji.js currently supports a whopping Emoji!

The newest additions are:

use

Place <script src="/path/to/emoji.js"></script> in your HTML file after your jQuery.

Place the {folder}Emoji your img folder and {document}Emoji.js in your js folder. Then take a look at the cheatsheet for available codes and get typing! All Emoji are written between {braces}.
